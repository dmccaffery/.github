# Support

Thanks for using our projects! This guide applies to every repository in the
[Bitwise Media Group][org] organisation and points you to the right place for what you need.

## Questions, ideas, and discussion

For **how-to questions, ideas, and open-ended conversation**, use
[GitHub Discussions][discussions] rather than the issue tracker. It keeps answers searchable
for the next person and keeps the issue tracker focused on tracked work — and we're happy to
help there.

## Bugs and feature requests

Found something broken, or have a concrete improvement in mind? **Open an issue** on the
affected repository and pick a template:

- **🐛 Bug report** — what happened, how to reproduce it, and what you expected.
- **✨ Feature request** — the problem you're trying to solve, not just the solution.

For anything beyond a small, obvious fix, please open an issue **before** sending a pull
request — it saves wasted effort and gives us a chance to point you in the right direction. See
[CONTRIBUTING.md][contributing] for the full workflow.

## Security

**Never report a vulnerability in a public issue or discussion.** Report it privately through
the affected repository's **Security** tab → _Report a vulnerability_. See our
[security policy][security] for details.
